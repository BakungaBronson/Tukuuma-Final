Who We Are

Uganda’s environment is deteriorating due to chronic pollution and the rapid destruction of natural resources. If not addressed, our country will waste away, and this is why TUKUUMA was conceived in March 2019 to provide sustainable ways of waste management.

TUKUUMA is a youth founded and led non-profit, volunteer based organisation committed to providing lasting solutions for the current environmental challenges of communities in Uganda and Africa.

What began with volunteering out of office time to make a positive difference in the environment of communities in Mukono District, is now a fully fledged, committed organisation that cares, nurtures and protects the environment.


Objectives

  • To facilitate, lead and advocate for environmental conservation and sustainability.
  • To skill and mentor youth and children to engage in the protection of the environment as a business or employment opportunity.
  • To facilitate communal cleanliness through sensitization and waste management skiliing and work-stations.
  • To advocate for and partner with non-government, government stakeholders and policy makers for the implementation of evidence-based environment protection policies and practices.

Our Story

Uganda’s environment is deteriorating due to chronic pollution and the rapid destruction of natural resources. If not addressed, our country will waste away, and this is why TUKUUMA was conceived in March 2019 to provide sustainable ways of waste management.

TUKUUMA is a youth founded and led non-profit, volunteer based organisation committed to providing lasting solutions for the current environmental challenges of communities in Uganda and Africa.

What began with volunteering out of office time to make a positive difference in the environment of communities in Mukono District, is now a fully fledged, committed organisation that cares, nurtures and protects the environment.

Mission

  • To facilitate, lead and advocate for environmental sustainability through partnership driven implementation of sustainable projects and research on alternative methods.

Vision

  • To foster sustainable environment and climate action practices, and natural communities through project implementation, youth involvement and research.

Values

  • MISSION CENTRED: We are deeply committed to the mission of sustainably protecting the natural environment
  • IMPACT DRIVEN: We do not stop until we made an impact in all our activities.
  • TRANSPARENCY: We believe transparency breeds trust, so we openly communicate our successes and failures.
  • PERSONAL GROWTH: We offer an environment of continuous growth where one can do, and become, their best.
  • FUN: We are proof that hard work and fun are compatible.

Current Projects

CLEAN UGANDA PROJECT (CUP)

  • In partnership with the Palle Moeller Foundation, we have purposed to clean and facilitate Kabanga Parish in to becoming a self sustaining, no waste community through; waste management education and training and skilling for recycling.

YOUTH ENVIRO-PRENEURSHIP PROJECT (YEPP)

  • Under this initiative we employ, skill, and mentor youth in applying business skills to protect the Environment.

TUKUUMA TREE PLANTING (TPP)

  • In partnership with Mercantile Credit Bank Limited, we have purposed to plant 10,000 P. africana trees every year in Mukono District and surrounding areas, for five years.
